Research

My research interests span Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Embodied AI, with particular focus on the following areas:

  • LLM agents. My research focuses on developing autonomous agents with sophisticated reasoning capabilities through self-improvement mechanisms and emergent behaviors.
  • Reinforcement Learning. I investigate novel RL algorithms and frameworks to optimize LLMs' decision-making processes and enhance their cognitive abilities in complex environments.
  • Embodied AI. I explore methodologies for developing embodied agents capable of continuous learning and adaptation through multi-modal interaction and experience accumulation.
